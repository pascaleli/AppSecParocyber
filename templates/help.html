How to make a transfer

  1. Go to Transfer.
  2. Search for the person you want to send money to (by name or username), or click a saved payee.
  3. Choose To account from the list (their account(s) will appear—e.g. “Main Checking ****4002”).
  4. Select From account (your account), enter the amount and optional memo, then click Send transfer.

Saved payees

On the Payees page you can add people you send money to often. They will appear as quick-select chips on the Transfer page. You can also add a payee when searching during a transfer.

Transactions

Dashboard shows your accounts. Click View on an account to see its transactions, or go to Transactions to see all activity across your accounts.

Contact

For support, contact your lab instructor or refer to the course materials.
